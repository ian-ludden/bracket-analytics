Vector definition:
Bits 00-14: West
Bits 15-29: East
Bits 30-44: Midwest
Bits 45-59: South(east)
Bit 60: West vs. East Final Four game (1 if West wins)
Bit 61: Midwest vs. South(east) Final Four game (1 if Midwest wins)
Bit 62: Championship game (1 if West/East wins)

Within each region's group of 15 bits:
(all are top to bottom)

First round is first 8 bits
Second round is next 4 bits
Regional semifinals are next 2 bits
Regional final: last bit

The first round is always:
1 vs. 16
8 vs. 9
5 vs. 12
4 vs. 13
6 vs. 11
3 vs. 14
7 vs. 10
2 vs. 15
